# Privacy Policy — Markdeck

**Last updated: June 2026**

subhbits ("we") built the Markdeck app as a free app. This privacy policy explains what data, if any, the app collects.

## Data We Collect

Markdeck does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

Specifically, we do not:
- Collect personal information
- Collect usage analytics or telemetry
- Send data to any server
- Use cookies or tracking
- Share any information with third parties

## Local Settings

Markdeck stores your file type preferences locally on your device using Windows Application Data storage. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.

## File Access

Markdeck opens files that you explicitly select or drag into the app. Files are processed entirely on your device. No file contents are transmitted anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

Markdeck does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

Markdeck is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
